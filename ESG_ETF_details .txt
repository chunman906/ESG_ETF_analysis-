For ESG ETF performance presentation:
- we're comparing the long term return between the combined markets return of ESG and Non-ESG ETF.
- It shows that ESG is slightly better but the difference is very small.
- However if we go dive into the specific market and specific , there are more difference.
- For the excess return it shows the ESG vs Non-ESG on each market, and we can see that the US and European markts deliver positive
  excess return while emerging and global markets are slightly neagative on that.
- If we compare different markets, the US and Europe also deliver higher return in 5 years.
- In here, we further zoom into the economic recession time during 2007-2009, so the ESG ETF in US market also performing slightly better, 
  and we can see the different economic cycling may have more difference on the ESG vs Non-ESG performance.
- If we look at each market, it seems that the European market is more different between ESG and Non ESG.
- In the correlation ratio comparision with Heatmap, the Europe market also has lower correlation.
- If we further look at S.D., the Europe market also has lowest volitility risk while US is the highest.
- Looking at Sharp ratio, the US ETF still performing the best as the returns is much higher, and if compare with 
  Information ratio we can see the US and European markets deliver better risk adjusted return performance.
